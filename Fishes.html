Fishes

Fishes are colorful aquatic animals kept in tanks or aquariums. They come in various shapes and sizes, from tiny neon tetras to large goldfish. Fish tanks require clean water, proper filtration, and regular feeding to keep the fishes healthy and happy.

Pet fishes are a great addition to a home as they are easy to care for and provide a calming presence.
However, they require regular maintenance and attention to ensure they stay healthy.
