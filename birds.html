Birds

Pet birds, like parrots and cockatiels, are popular for their intelligence and ability to mimic speech. They require spacious cages, varied diets, and social interaction to thrive. Species such as budgies (parakeets) are colorful and known for their playful demeanor, making them enjoyable companions for bird enthusiasts.


  • There are many types of pet birds, each with different needs and characteristics.
  • Birds need proper care, including a balanced diet, clean living environment, and regular social interaction.
